Guacamole

Description

Guacamole is a fresh avocado dip that is creamy, bright, and packed with flavor.

It’s perfect with tortilla chips, tacos, or as a side for any Mexican-style meal.

Ingredients

  • Ripe avocados
  • Lime juice
  • Salt
  • Red onion (finely chopped)
  • Tomato (diced)
  • Cilantro (chopped)
  • Jalapeño (optional)

Steps

  1. Mash avocados in a bowl.
  2. Stir in lime juice and salt.
  3. Add onion, tomato, cilantro, and jalapeño (if using).
  4. Taste and adjust seasoning.
  5. Serve immediately.
