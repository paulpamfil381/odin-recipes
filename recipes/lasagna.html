Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna is a layered pasta dish made with a rich meat sauce, creamy cheese, and tender noodles baked until bubbly.

It is a comfort-food staple and works well for family dinners, meal prep, and gatherings.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 190°C (375°F).
  2. Cook the noodles according to the package instructions, then drain.
  3. Cook ground beef with onion and garlic, then add tomato sauce and simmer.
  4. Layer sauce, noodles, ricotta, and mozzarella in a baking dish; repeat.
  5. Top with mozzarella and parmesan.
  6. Bake for 30–40 minutes, until hot and bubbly.
  7. Rest 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
