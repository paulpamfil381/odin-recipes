Pancakes

Description

These pancakes are soft, fluffy, and quick to make with basic pantry ingredients.

Serve them warm with butter and your favorite toppings like syrup, fruit, or yogurt.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Butter (melted)

Steps

  1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Whisk in milk, egg, and melted butter until just combined.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled pan over medium heat.
  4. Pour batter onto the pan and cook until bubbles form on top.
  5. Flip and cook until golden brown.
  6. Serve immediately.
